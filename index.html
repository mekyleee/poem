Months Gone By

--Mekyle

Weeks had gone, Months had passed
Days had flown, yet we stand,
Hand to hand, heart to heart.
Memories itches, in love’s sweets name
Moments shine, in one person’s name.
Sometimes we’ve talked from day to night,
from afternoon sunlight, to midnight moonlight.
Sometimes we’ve even whispered talks, through the night.
Mostly we laugh, sometimes we do our little fights,
In which it will be fixed that night.
Through all the laughter and trials we’ve shared
There’s a bond that builds, strengthens and stronger.
"A HAPPY MONTHSARY FOR THE BOTH OF US."
The first mark of the month is just a start,
Of the beginning, without seeing its end.
--Mekyle
