Short Story: Cycle of Endings

In a land beyond mountains,
there was an animal
who's born with everything
its gaze is as fierce as lighting,
its claws is as sharp as sword
a body that's made for hunting
He made no friends, and even left his family
thinking everyone around him wil be just a burden
For he think he's different.
It hunt and kills its prey, naive and bold with no pity in his eyes.
Sometimes it just kills its prey for the sake of fun.
its fierce is untoppable.
As years passed by, the animal became old but fierce as ever.
he's hungry so he hunts for food, as he saw his prey and run towards it
he wasn't be able to catch it.
it was the day, he realized that he become slow, fragile and weak.
for once a predator now a prey
once a hunter now become hunted
and that signifies its destruction.
